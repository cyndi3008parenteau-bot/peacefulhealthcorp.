Our Staffing Model

The home operates with 4 core staff: 1 LPN, 1 Registered Social Worker, and 2 HCAs. Staff work 12-hour shifts on a 4-days-on/4-days-off rotation. Relief staff are available as needed to ensure continuous, high-quality care.

Our Team Responsibilities

Medical & Health Care

Our Licensed Practical Nurse provides medical oversight, medication management, specialized foot care, and daily health monitoring to ensure the well-being of all residents.

Social Work & Counseling

Our Registered Social Worker provides personalized care coordination, counseling, and support services, drawing on extensive experience with vulnerable populations and special needs clients.

Daily Living Support

Our Health Care Aides assist residents with daily activities, personal care, and mobility support, creating a safe and comfortable environment where residents feel valued.

Life Skills & Recreation

Our team provides life skills training and facilitates participation in recreational and social activities to promote independence and social engagement among residents.
