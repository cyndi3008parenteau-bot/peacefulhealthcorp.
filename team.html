Meet Our Professional Team

At Peaceful Healthcare Corp, our team consists of 4 dedicated professionals: 1 Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN), 1 Registered Social Worker, and 2 Health Care Aides (HCAs) who bring extensive experience and specialized skills to provide exceptional residential care and support services.

Our Staffing Model

The home operates with 4 core staff: 1 LPN, 1 Registered Social Worker, and 2 HCAs. Staff work 12-hour shifts on a 4-days-on/4-days-off rotation. Relief staff are available as needed to ensure continuous, high-quality care.
